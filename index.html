Racial Discrimination

January 13, 2021



What is this about?

Racial Discrimination involves unjust treatment to an individual because of his/her race, skin color, or ethnic origin. Because of this, their human rights are violated.
In our world today, discrimination against people of color continue to get out of hand. Being looked down on and treated unreasonably because of one's race is a major problem in our society. Because of this, people have started to protest against the system just for their voices to be heard. This can affect them physically and mentally. In addition, this had already cost a lot of lives. Despite the recent events, others still don't see this as an issue.
