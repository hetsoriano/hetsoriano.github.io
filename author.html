2nd Quarter Project


Hi! I am Hannah, a grade 9 student. I know my website is not perfect, but it was an experience that helped me gain more knowledge on HTML and CSS.

This website is our project for Computer Science 3. Here, we showcased our Satirical Essay composition in English 3. I chose to write about Racial discrimination since this is one of the most serious matters we should address. Because of one's race, people do not treat each other equally. It even reached the point where several innocent lives are lost. I have no intention of executing the proposal, but I wanted others to be informed how much problems this is causing.
